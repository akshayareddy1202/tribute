His self-sacrifice through a hunger strike led to the formation of the state of Andhra Pradesh, which was carved out for Telugu-speaking people.

Sriramulu's dedication to the cause of linguistic and cultural identity inspired millions and highlighted the importance of recognizing regional languages and cultures in a diverse country like India. His life and work continue to be celebrated for their impact on Indian federalism and unity.
